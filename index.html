05 CONCLUSION

Conclusion and Insights on Land Ownership Survey

                       This survey helped us understand how people feel about land ownership in their area. We learned that many people find it hard to check who owns land or get correct land records. Some people also shared that they have faced problems like missing or wrong information.Through this survey, we gained valuable insights into the perceptions and challenges people face regarding land ownership in the area. Many respondents expressed difficulty in accessing accurate land records or identifying legal landowners. Several also mentioned issues such as outdated or incorrect information in official records, highlighting the need for a more transparent and reliable land management system.


                       Even those who don’t own land had useful ideas and knew who owns land near them. This shows that a better system is needed—one that is easy to use, gives correct information, and helps everyone. Our team will use this information to build a digital tool that makes land ownership clear and simple for everyone.Interestingly, even individuals who do not personally own land demonstrated awareness of local land ownership patterns and contributed useful suggestions. This indicates a strong community interest in improving access to land information. The survey results clearly show the demand for a user-friendly, accurate digital system that allows citizens to verify land ownership details easily. Our team aims to develop such a tool to promote fairness and reduce disputes related to land.

                       Finally, based on the data collected, we found that 38% of the surveyed individuals own land, 44% do not own any land, and 18% have sold their land. These statistics highlight the need for policies or systems that can support people without land, possibly through government schemes or land redistribution programs. The findings will guide our future work in building a digital solution that brings transparency, equity, and efficiency to land ownership tracking...
